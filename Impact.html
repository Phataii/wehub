Heifer International

Vision: To end hunger and poverty while caring for the Earth.

Our Work With Heifer International:

Program Name: Business Development Service to Nimsy Agro Solar Concept

Objective:

To develop internal capabilities within Nimsy Agro, improving operational efficiency, enhancing strategic clarity, and increasing readiness for growth and external partnerships. This was achieved through a comprehensive Business Development Support (BDS) engagement, including organizational capacity diagnostic, structured training, handholding, and system implementation sessions.

Our Role:

Wennovation Hub implemented the BDS engagement, providing support across multiple functional areas, including finance, sales, marketing, performance management, human resources, and branding, to ensure Nimsy Agro's holistic development.

Key Achievements:
  • Nimsy Agro now operates with a clearer organisational structure.
  • Improved documentation processes.
  • Standardised sales and customer engagement workflows.
  • Better digital visibility.

The BDS engagement by Wennovation Hub, supported by Heifer International, has fundamentally transformed our operations at Nimsy Agro, setting us on a clear path for sustainable growth.

Locations: Africa, USA
Established: Partner since 2023
Email: info@heifer.org

Crosswise Works

Vision: To promote sustainable international development through entrepreneurship.

Our Work With Crosswise Works:

Program Name: The Next Economy Program

Objective:

To empower young people in Nigeria, Mali, Somalia, and Somaliland with the skills and confidence for successful careers or entrepreneurial ventures.

Our Role:

Crosswise Works deployed Wennovation Hub as the implementing partner for The Next Economy program. We were responsible for the direct execution of program phases, including entrepreneurship training and incubation, leveraging our local expertise and network to ensure deep impact at the grassroots level.

Key Achievements:
  • Successfully implemented key phases of The Next Economy program on the ground.
  • Directly engaged young participants in skill development and venture creation.
  • Contributed to the program's overall success in fostering youth entrepreneurship.

This partnership highlights our capacity as a trusted implementing partner for large-scale international development initiatives.
